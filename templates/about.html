🌾 About AI Crop Advisor

Smart Farming Powered by Artificial Intelligence

🎯 Project Objective

AI Crop Advisor is an intelligent agriculture assistant designed to support farmers, agriculture students, and agricultural professionals by providing accurate and practical farming guidance using Artificial Intelligence.

🌱 Key Benefits

  • 24/7 AI-powered farming assistance
  • Crop recommendation support
  • Fertilizer and nutrient guidance
  • Disease identification and treatment suggestions
  • Weather information integration
  • English and Kannada language support
  • Voice-enabled interaction
  • Mobile-friendly responsive design

🚀 Future Enhancements

  • Crop disease detection using image upload
  • Satellite-based crop monitoring
  • Advanced weather forecasting
  • Market price prediction
  • Farmer dashboard and analytics
  • AI-powered personalized crop planning
